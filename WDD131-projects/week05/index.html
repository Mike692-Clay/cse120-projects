Site Name

Daily Inspiration Quotes – This name reflects the website’s purpose: providing uplifting, motivational quotes each day to encourage positivity and self-growth.

Optional domain availability: daily-inspiration.org

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to share inspirational quotes from famous thinkers, leaders, and writers. It will serve as a hub of positivity by providing daily motivation, categorized quotes (such as happiness, success, perseverance), and user-submitted quotes for community engagement.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find daily quotes to help me start my morning with positivity?
  • Can I search for quotes based on themes such as success, perseverance, or happiness?

Color Schema

Parchment Beige (#fdf6e3): Background for the page (old paper feel).

Black Ink (#1a1a1a): Body text (classic ink look).

Darker Parchment (#e6d8b6): Header and footer background.

Dark Brown Ink (#3b2f2f): Headings for emphasis.

Typography

  • Garamond – used for headings (classic, elegant serif font).
  • Georgia – used for body text (easy to read, traditional feel).
  • Courier New – optional accent for quotes, giving them a typewriter look.
